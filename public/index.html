Samvad version-(0.0.3)

This is an online chat platform for real-time communication. This makes it easy to chat privately with a group or stay in the public space for open conversation.

It supports multiple rooms for group conversations. Each room is private — only members of that room can see its messages and member names.

If you do not enter a room name at login, you will automatically join the public room. If you enter a room name, you will join or create that specific room.
